ABOUT US

We started in 2012 by opening a school for children from low-income communities in Mumbai. Our experience in running the school led us to understand the value that high quality teaching brings to a child's learning in school.


The urgency for having more confident and empowered educators across schools led us to build customized programs for our teachers.
